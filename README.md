# Customer Service Data Analysis Turning VOC into Business Insights

A structured analysis of a multi-country customer service dataset, focusing on product issues, customer feedback (VOC), and complaint resolution. This project demonstrates data cleaning, transformation, and insight generation using Excel to support decision-making across product, IT, HR, and customer support teams.

---

### Data Cleaning & Preparation
- Translated Voice of Customer (VOC) feedback into English  
- Extracted key fields using the Excel `TEXT` function  
- Reduced dataset from **20 columns to 13 relevant columns**  
- Structured data for efficient analysis  

---

### Analysis Focus
The analysis was structured to provide insights across multiple business functions, including Product, IT, HR, and Customer Support.

---

### Key Analysis Areas

#### 1. Issue Volume by Product
- Identified products generating the highest number of issues  
- Highlights potential revenue loss areas  
- Supports product improvement and testing priorities  

#### 2. Top Affected Countries
- Identified regions with high complaint volumes  
- Supports:
  - Stronger quality control  
  - Targeted product testing  
- Helps improve performance across markets  

#### 3. Customer Feedback Analysis
- **27% of customers were dissatisfied**  
- Slow response time identified as a key issue  

**Business Value:**
- Supports HR in identifying performance gaps  
- Enables targeted training and service improvement  

#### 4. Resolved Cases Analysis
- Faster resolution:
  - Wireless mouse  
  - USB hub  

- Slower resolution:
  - Bluetooth speaker  
  - Laptop stand  
  - Gaming keyboard  

**Business Value:**
- Highlights areas needing improved support processes  
- Supports IT in understanding product complexity  
- Guides improvements in pre-release testing  

#### 5. Ticket Status Analysis
- Categorized tickets into:
  - Escalated  
  - Pending  
  - Resolved  

**Business Value:**
- Provides visibility into support efficiency  
- Helps track unresolved issues and service bottlenecks  

---

### Overall Business Impact
- Improves product quality through issue identification  
- Enhances customer satisfaction by addressing key pain points  
- Supports HR with performance and training insights  
- Enables IT to prioritize product testing and fixes  
- Provides leadership with clear visibility into service performance  

---

### Tools Used
- **Excel**: Data cleaning, transformation, analysis, and visualization  
- Functions: TEXT  
- Pivot Tables and Charts

### Conclusion
This project demonstrates how structured customer service data analysis can uncover operational gaps, improve service delivery, and support data-driven decision-making across multiple teams.
