# Customer Service Data Analysis and Dashboard
Customer service data analysis using Excel to evaluate product issues, customer feedback, and resolution performance across countries. Provides insights on complaint trends, response efficiency, and product-related issues to support business, IT, and HR decision-making.

### Overview
This project analyzes a customer service dataset covering product issues, customer feedback, and complaint resolution across multiple countries and languages. The goal is to transform raw data into actionable insights that support decision-making across departments.

---

### Data Preparation
- Translated Voice of Customer (VOC) feedback into English  
- Extracted key fields using the Excel TEXT function  
- Reduced dataset from 20 columns to 13 relevant fields  
- Structured data for efficient analysis  

---

### Analysis Areas
- Issue volume by product  
- Top affected countries  
- Customer feedback and satisfaction  
- Resolution performance by product  
- Ticket status distribution (Escalated, Pending, Resolved)  

---

### Key Insights
- Certain products generate higher issue volumes, indicating potential revenue risk  
- Specific countries show higher complaint concentration, highlighting gaps in quality control  
- 27% of customers reported dissatisfaction, with slow response time as a key issue  
- Wireless mouse and USB hub issues were resolved faster  
- Bluetooth speaker, laptop stand, and gaming keyboard had slower resolution rates  

---

### Business Impact
- **Product Improvement**: Identifies high-issue products for better testing and quality enhancement  
- **Customer Experience**: Highlights dissatisfaction drivers (e.g., slow response), enabling service improvement  
- **Operational Efficiency**: Reveals resolution gaps to improve support processes and turnaround time  
- **Regional Strategy**: Pinpoints countries with high complaints for targeted interventions  
- **Team Alignment**: Supports IT, HR, and Customer Support with data-driven insights for decision-making  

---

### Tools Used
- **Excel**: Data cleaning, transformation, analysis, and visualization  
- Functions: TEXT  
- Pivot Tables and Charts  

---

### Conclusion
This project demonstrates how structured customer service data analysis can improve product quality, enhance customer satisfaction, and drive better operational and strategic decisions across the organization.
